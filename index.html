Web Design &
Development
Course

The Web Design and Development course provides
comprehensive training in creating visually
appealing and functional websites. The course covers essential
topics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive design principles.
Students will learn to design user-friendly
interfaces, develop dynamic web applications,
and integrate multimedia elements.
With hands-on projects and real-world applications, this
course equips learners with the skills needed to excel in the rapidly
evolving field of web design and development.
